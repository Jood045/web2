Banana Oat Pancakes
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup oats
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon (optional)
  1. Mash the banana and mix with the egg
  2. Add oats and cinnamon
  3. Cook small pancakes on a non-stick pan
